How it works

Buy or Rent a Property in four easy steps

1 - SEARCH:

By using our super search form, you get access to variety, quality, convinient Real estate property offerings. With varying prices, all you have to do is select the most appropriate based on your needs.

2 - EXPLORE:

After getting your search result, you can select individual searched results to explore and see more details about the property. You get to see the location of the property on map, structural details of the property, prices and access to the property agent.

3 - BUY/RENT:

Buying or Renting is easy. Just fill out the purchase form and you are good to go. The property agent will immediately contact you to prepare you to get settled in your newly aquired property.

4 - YOUR ABODE:

With transaction finally completed, graciously move to your new property. Visit how blog to see how we select a location-aware property for you. And also how to best customize you search.

  • Location-aware search

    Search are location-aware, prioritize based on your location. Search results can also be tailored to meet your specific needs.

  • Variety of options

    Select your preference from a list of options at various prices that match your budget and property specification.

  • Quick payment and relocation

    Pay conviniently via our secure and robust payment system. You can also request our courier service at a very affordable price to ease your relocation. For more information, you can read our Terms of service here.
